How to Play

  • Divide players into two teams (Red and Blue).
  • Select a category and difficulty level.
  • Start the timer when the actor begins acting.
  • Each card shows its difficulty level (green=easy=1 point, blue=medium=2 points, red=hard=3 points).
  • Correct guesses earn your team points based on difficulty.
  • When the timer runs out, it's the other team's turn.
  • The team with the most points when all cards are completed wins!
